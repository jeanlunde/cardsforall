About

Join us at our creative holiday event. We'll be making holiday cards for distribution to patients at Gillette Children's Healthcare. Young families, children, and all ages are welcome. We'll supply the art materials and a variety of media. Show your creativity and support your local independent business by purchasing coffee and refreshments.

We'll supply card examples or you can create your own.
Who we are: We're students in Century College's Visual Communication Technology (VCT) department who want to share a creative holiday event. Visit us on Facebook, and contact us if you have any questions.
